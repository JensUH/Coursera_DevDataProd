Sales prediction based on marketing spending by Company X

Jens

Needs

Company X needs to improve its sales numbers.

Company X currently devide its marketing budget across advertisement in TV, Radio and Dailies.

Company X currently have two competitors who runs advertisement campaigns as well.

However, there are no current knowledge of what effect these different advertisements have on sales.

Thus, knowledge of how to optimize the marketing budget to attain highest possible Return On Investment is needed.

Approach

Using previous sales and marketing data from (fictitious) Company X to predict how future sales will be driven by spending on different media groups.

The following Marketing Mix Model is used:

Sales <- Base + 0.54*Adstock(TV, 0.7) + 0.61*Adstock(Radio, 0.6) +
     0.34*Adstock(Dailies, 0.78) - 0.32*Adstock(Competitor1, 0.72) -
     0.17*Adstock(Competitor2, 0.73)

## Error in eval(expr, envir, enclos): object 'Base' not found

The function Adstock, is a transformation of the funds spend on a media that takes care of the fact that:

  1. Media spending in one week will same some effect in the following week ("spill-over effect")
  2. There is a limit to how high sales can be increased by advertisement in a particular media

Benefits

Using the constructed Shiny App, Company X will be able to see the predicted effect on sales of their media allocation budget for the next four weeks.

With sliders, the spending on Tv, Radio, and Dailies in the next four weeks can be set.

The following is then reported:

  1. The total medial spending the next four weeks
  2. The predicted total sales the next for weeks
  3. The predicted Return on Investment

A nice plot is added give a visual understanding of different allocation.
